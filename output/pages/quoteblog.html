Quoteblog

For years I've compiled index cards of quotes that have made an impression on me. At first I kept them on the back of my bedroom door so that they were the first thing I saw when I woke up in the morning. A few month's ago I stumbled onto Aaron Schwartz's quoteblog Raw Meat. Almost a decade after making the first card, I've decided to open source my quote collection. I hope they inspire you just as they have inspired me. Cheers!

The unexamined life is not worth living.
- Socrates

I am the wisest man alive for I know one thing, and that is that I know nothing.
- Socrates

Be a philosopher - but amidst all your philosophy, be still a man.
- David Hume

A failure to plan is a plan for failure.
- Anon

Let the future tell the truth and evaluate each one according to his works and accomplishments. The present is theirs; the future, for which I have really worked, is mine.

On Discovery

The noblest pleasure is the joy of understanding.
- Leonardo Da Vinci
